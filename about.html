Technology solutions for development problems

The Technology and Development Solutions Cell (TDSC) is a resource center for providing technology-based solutions for the development sector. TDSC provides resources and services to undertake projects that deliver outputs via academic research outputs, consulting, outreach modules, entrepreneurship guidance, etc. TDSC not only aims at delivering solutions to external stakeholders but primarily uses the academic platform to engage students directly in development-related problems and solutions. The resources of the center can be leveraged by faculty and students of IIT Bombay and other colleges to provide solutions to the community and various collaborators and partners.

TDSC has been in operation in 2013, and has worked with government departments, gram panchayats, NGOs, academic institutions, industry donors. Nature of our projects have spanned technical assessment and audit, design, case studies, content generation and training etc.

The TDSC team comprises of faculty members of IIT Bombay, a 10+ strong team of full time members (Bachelors and Masters), students of IIT Bombay and student interns.
