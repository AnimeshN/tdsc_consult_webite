Watershed interventions for habitations in Kurlod and Botoshi, Mokhada taluka

Status : Completed

Project Background:

The Kurlod-Botoshi watershed project is a joint collaboration between Aroehan, Siemens CSR and TDSC, which aims to increase water availability for both drinking and livelihood opportunities in the 13 habitations of Kurlod and Botoshi in Mokhada taluka. In this project, TDSC provided technical guidance towards planning, design and implementation of watershed interventions, Aroehan is managing the on-site implementation, and Siemens assisted with funding and overall steering. The project was implemented over three years, with three rounds of implementation (Jan-March 2015 and Jan-March 2016 and Jan-June 2017) The project is almost complete, with technical support during on-field construction being done since May2017.
