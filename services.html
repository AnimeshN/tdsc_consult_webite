Services

Home Services
TDSC offers technical consulting services on projects serving the bottom 80% in the basic sectors of water, energy, sanitation, healthcare and agriculture. For existing systems, we offer status assessment, redesign, monitoring and evaluation services. For planned/new projects, we offer need-assessment, techno-economic feasibility study, detailed design, and monitoring of implementation.
service 01

Need-assessment and pre-feasibility analysis

Quantitative study of a basic needs problem at a specific location, and an exploration of possible solutions

Execution cycle: 1-3 months.

For Example: Drinking water stress assessment for a gram panchayat

view all project
service 02

Third-party audit and evaluation

Neutral third-party evaluation of an existing or planned scheme/programme/project (generally based on technical design, economic feasibility, social factors)

Execution cycle: 2-4 months

For Example: Energy audit of a town's water supply scheme; social audit of a major CSR project

view all project
service 03

Feasibility assessment and design

Detailed feasibility study and technical design for new assets

Execution cycle: 4-6 months

For Example: Design of a rainwater harvesting system for a rural PHC; design of a single-village rural solar pumping system for irrigation

view all project
service 04

Design and execution of case studies

Development of case studies highlighting best practices, challenges and solutions for problem areas in the key sectors

Execution cycle: 2-6 months

For Example: Case studies on community-based water treatment solutions for the urban and local poor

view all project
service 05

Training and outreach

Preparation of training materials for various stakeholders (e.g. students, NGOs, pump operators) on best practices, design protocols, tools (e.g. GIS)

Execution cycle: 2-3 months

For Example: Training session for ULB staff and engineers on the use of basic GIS tools for better city planning

view all project
service 06

Gadget/device prototype development

Design or redesign of devices or gadgets that may serve one of the basic needs of the bottom 80% (energy, water, healthcare, agriculture, livelihood etc)

Execution cycle: 2-6 months

For Example: Design of a low-cost CVD diagnostic tool for rural PHCs
